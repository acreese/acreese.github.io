Georgia Tech Executive Dashboards

Data management and dashboard creation for Georgia Tech’s Professional Education Portfolio Development.

Python | Tableau

Georgia Tech Professional Education (GTPE) creates for-credit credentials and courses for online Master of Science and Professional Master degree programs as well non-credit courses requested by outside organizations. Until now, GTPE had no structured intake process for evaluating and prioritizing these credentials and courses. My capstone project contributed to the development and execution of this intake process through data flow diagramming, data wrangling with Python, and data visualization with Tableau to create data dashboards for external requestors to track project progress and for internal executives to support their intake decisions with data on course business value, resource requirements, and risks.
